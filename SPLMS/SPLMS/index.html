    Our Humble Beginnings

    In our Software Project Lab 2, our supervisor inspires us to develop a system that can help to manage the course. From that motivation we started to develop the system. In the system the course manager can create academic calendar and invite the teacher to create group. The teacher create groups and add students. The students can upload their document before presentation and the teachers can give feedbacks of the presentation. The manager upload the mark sheet of the groups.
